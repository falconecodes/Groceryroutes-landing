Privacy.

Short version: We collect as little as possible. We don't sell your data. Your grocery lists are yours.

What we collect

Zip code (to show local stores), selected stores, grocery lists, and trip totals. We use Apple's standard analytics for crash and usage (opt-in).

What we don't collect

Name, address, phone, payment info, contacts, photos, location background tracking, or any other identifying data. We don't need it to compare prices.

Sharing

We never sell your data. We never share your data with stores, advertisers, or third parties for marketing.

Storage

Lists and savings live on your device and optionally sync through iCloud. We do not maintain a central user database.

Your rights

Delete the app and your data is gone. CCPA/GDPR equivalent access/deletion requests: email groceryroutes@gmail.com. We reply within 30 days.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll flag it in-app and on this page. Contact: groceryroutes@gmail.com.
